About Us

We are a family-owned pizza restaurant that specializes in making delicious and fresh pizza using only the finest ingredients. We take pride in providing our customers with the best dining experience possible, whether they choose to dine-in, take-out, or have their order delivered to their doorstep.

Our menu offers a wide range of pizza options, including classic Margherita, pepperoni, meat lovers, and vegetarian, as well as specialty pizzas like our BBQ chicken and Hawaiian pizza. We also have a variety of appetizers, salads, pasta dishes, and desserts to satisfy every taste bud.

At our pizza shop, we strive to offer high-quality food and excellent customer service to all of our customers, and we look forward to serving you soon!

Delivery Information

We offer free delivery within a 5-mile radius of our restaurant. Orders can be placed online, by phone, or through one of our delivery partners such as Grubhub, DoorDash, or Uber Eats.

Please allow 30-45 minutes for delivery. Our drivers are equipped with insulated bags to keep your pizza hot and fresh during transit.

If you have any special delivery instructions, please let us know when placing your order.
